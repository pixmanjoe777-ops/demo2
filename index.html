Our story

A neighborhood salon
run like a favorite restaurant.

Linh Tran opened Heavenly Nails in 2004 on a quiet stretch of Waukegan Road with a simple idea — treat every pair of hands like they belonged to a friend. Twenty-two years later, most of our book is still neighbors.

We use fume-extracted stations, hospital-grade sterilization, and polish lines we'd put on our own kids — because most of our guests come with theirs.
