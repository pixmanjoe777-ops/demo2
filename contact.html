Good to know

Questions, answered.

Do I need an appointment?

Walk-ins are always welcome, but weekends and Fridays book up — we recommend reserving online or calling a day or two ahead.

How early should I arrive?

Five minutes early is plenty. If you're a new guest, ten — we'll walk you through the polish library and chat about what you're after.

What if I need to cancel?

A quick text to (847) 486-4131 any time more than two hours before your appointment is fine — no fee.

Do you do nails for weddings or events?

Yes. Book a consultation with Thuy at least two weeks out. We can coordinate a bridal party in-studio or travel for groups of six or more.

Are kids welcome?

Absolutely — we have a princess polish shelf and do sized-down services for under-12s. We also have a quiet corner if a parent is the one getting pampered.

Is the studio accessible?

Yes — street-level entrance, accessible restroom, and we can bring a pedicure chair to a standard-height seat if needed.

What polish brands do you carry?

OPI, DND, The Gel Bottle, and a rotating shelf of five-free brands. If you have a favorite, bring it — we're happy to use your own.

Do you accept tips on card?

Yes, though cash and Venmo (@heavenlynails-glenview) are most appreciated by the team.
